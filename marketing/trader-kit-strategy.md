# Porfilr — Trader Kit: product spec + distribution

The wedge: traders need to prove a CREDIBLE, VERIFIABLE track record to get funded,
attract investors, land prop-firm attention, or sell signals/mentorship. Today they send
screenshots (easily faked, zero credibility) or raw MyFXBook links (ugly, not a pitch).
No competitor offers the *beautiful, credible, shareable portfolio layer* on top of verified data.

Positioning: **"MyFXBook is your proof. Porfilr is your pitch."**
Tagline: *"Turn your track record into a page that gets you funded."*

---

## Who it's for
Retail traders, prop-firm (FTMO-type) funded traders, forex/crypto/futures traders, signal
providers, trading educators/mentors, aspiring fund managers. Their goal: capital, clients,
credibility. Higher willingness to pay than a hobbyist freelancer — they're monetizing.

## Their pain (what to attack in copy)
- Screenshots look amateur and fake-able → investors don't trust them
- MyFXBook/FXBlue are verification tools, not a professional presentation
- Track record scattered across Telegram, Discord, DMs
- No single credible link to send an investor or prop firm

---

## What goes in the kit (the sections that make it undeniable)
1. **Verified track record hero** — key metrics up front: total return %, win rate, profit
   factor, max drawdown, timeframe. The numbers an investor scans first.
2. **Verification badge / link** — link or embed MyFXBook / FXBlue / broker statement, with a
   "Verified via ___" badge. Credibility is the entire game — this is the #1 differentiator.
3. **Equity curve chart** — the growth curve that sells (image upload or embed to start).
4. **Strategy summary** — what they trade, style (scalp/swing/position), timeframe, in plain
   language an investor understands.
5. **Risk profile** — max drawdown, risk per trade, risk-management rules. Investors buy risk
   control, not just returns.
6. **Monthly / yearly returns table** — consistency over time.
7. **Markets & instruments** — forex, indices, crypto, futures.
8. **Credentials** — years trading, prop-firm badges (FTMO funded, etc.), certifications.
9. **Services / offer** — managed accounts, signals, mentorship, copy-trading.
10. **Contact / "work with me" CTA** — Porfilr's built-in contact form → turns the page into
    deal flow (invest with me / join my signals). THIS is the lead-capture wedge.
11. **Testimonials** — from investors / students.
12. **Compliant risk disclaimer** — pre-built. Signals professionalism + covers them legally.

## Why it beats everything else
- MyFXBook/FXBlue = data & verification, but ugly and not a brand/pitch.
- Linktree/generic builders = no trading-specific credibility features.
- Porfilr Trader Kit = the **credible, beautiful, shareable pitch page that sits on top of
  verified data AND captures leads.** Nobody owns this space.

# Distribution — how a niche SOLVES it (not worsens it)

A niche makes distribution EASIER: the audience is concentrated, reachable, has influencers,
and a message only they feel. "Everyone" has no community to go to; traders do.

## 1. Warm start (do this first)
The traders already asking you = your seed. Onboard them free/cheap, make them your first
case studies, testimonials, and referrers. Traders follow and talk to each other — word of
mouth in this community is strong.

## 2. Where traders gather (go to them)
- **Fintwit (X trading community)** — huge, active, already posting track records publicly
- **Prop-firm communities** — FTMO/funded-trader Discords & forums (these traders NEED a
  credible page → perfect fit)
- **Trading Discords / Telegram groups**
- **Reddit** — r/Forex, r/Daytrading, r/algotrading, r/FuturesTrading
- **MyFXBook community** — verified traders already congregate there
- **YouTube / TikTok trading niche**

## 3. The highest-leverage channel: influencer + prop-firm partnerships
Trading has MASSIVE influencer ecosystems (educators, signal sellers) whose entire audience
needs this. This is your best shot:
- Give 3–5 trading influencers the kit free + an **affiliate cut**, they promote to their
  audience of traders who all need a credible page.
- **Prop firms** want funded traders to look credible → a partnership ("build your funded-
  trader page") is a real channel. Ambitious but high-upside.

## 4. The native hook (per channel)
> "Stop sending screenshots. Get a verified track record page that gets you funded."

Sharp, native, and only a trader feels it. Use it in communities, ads, and influencer briefs.

## The channel to OWN
**Fintwit/X + trading-influencer partnerships**, powered by the warm inbound you already have.
Concentrated, influencer-heavy, publicly track-record-obsessed. Own this before adding others.

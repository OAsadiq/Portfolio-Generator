# Porfilr — Video Editor Brief

Welcome, and thanks for working on Porfilr videos. This doc has everything you need to
start fast. If anything's unclear, just ask — short questions beat guessing.

---

## What Porfilr is (10-second version)
Porfilr is a tool that lets freelancers and creators build a professional portfolio
website in ~10 minutes — no code, no designer. The pitch: *"Stop sending Google Docs.
Build a real portfolio that gets you hired."*

Audience: freelancers, designers, developers, photographers, writers. Tone: confident,
clean, a little bold — never corporate or salesy.

---

## What I need from you
Short vertical videos for **TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts**.
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical, 1080×1920
- **Length:** 15–30 seconds (shorter is better)
- **Deliver:** MP4, plus a square 1:1 export if it's easy
- **From:** scripts I provide + brand graphics + screen-recordings of the app

---

## The #1 rule: the first 2 seconds
Most people scroll in 2 seconds. The opening hook (big text + motion) has to stop the
scroll. If the first frame is boring, nothing else matters. Lead with the hook line from
the script, big and bold, immediately.

---

## Style guide
**Colors (brand):**
- Orange (primary accent): `#ea580c` — also `#fb923c` for brighter highlights
- Dark background: `#0c0a09` / `#1c1917`
- Off-white text: `#fafaf9`
- Keep it mostly dark background + white text + orange accents. Clean, high-contrast.

**Fonts:** Bold sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or similar). Big, punchy headlines.

**Captions:** ALWAYS add captions/subtitles — most people watch muted. Make them large,
high-contrast, word-by-word or short phrases. This is non-negotiable.

**Pacing:** Fast cuts, no dead air. Text appears in sync with the beat/voiceover. Energy
without feeling frantic.

**Music:** Trending, upbeat but not distracting. Low volume under any voiceover. Use
royalty-free / platform-native audio.

**Logo:** "Porfilr" wordmark with an orange "r". End every video with the logo +
"porfilr.com".

---

## Do's and don'ts
DO:
- Hook in the first 2 seconds
- Captions on everything
- End on the CTA: "porfilr.com" / "Build yours free"
- Keep it short — cut anything that drags
- Match the brand colors above

DON'T:
- No stock-corporate vibes or cheesy transitions
- No tiny text (it must read on a phone)
- Don't bury the hook — it goes first, not after an intro
- Don't add a long logo intro — get to the value immediately
- Don't misspell anything (this is a credibility product — double-check text)

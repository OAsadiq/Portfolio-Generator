## Job posting (paste this)

**Title:** Growth & Social Marketer (Part-time / Contract)

**About Porfilr**
Porfilr is a no-code portfolio builder for freelancers and creators — build a professional
portfolio in 10 minutes. We just launched, we have a real product and early users, and a
content team (video + graphics + motion). What we're missing is someone to actually get
those creations in front of people and turn attention into signups.

**The role**
Own our distribution and engagement. You'll run our channels, engage our audience daily,
run email campaigns, and coordinate our content creators — all pointed at one goal: more
people signing up at porfilr.com. This is a growth role, not a "post pretty pictures" role.

**What you'll do**
- Run and grow our X, Instagram, and TikTok (posting, but mostly *engaging* — replies, DMs, communities)
- Turn our existing graphics/videos/scripts into a consistent posting cadence
- Run email campaigns to our list (we use Resend; list is already growing)
- Find and work relevant communities (Reddit, Indie Hackers, freelance/design groups)
- Track what works using our analytics (Cloudflare + an in-house events dashboard) and double down
- Brief our video editor + designers on what to make next based on what's converting

**You're a fit if you**
- Have grown a social account or a product's audience **from near-zero** (show us the numbers)
- Care about **signups and traffic**, not vanity likes
- Are proactive, self-managing, and comfortable engaging in DMs/communities
- Know email marketing basics (segmentation, subject lines, deliverability)
- Bonus: experience marketing to freelancers/creators/SaaS

**Logistics**
- Part-time / contract to start, remote, flexible hours
- 1-month paid trial, then ongoing if it's working
- You'll get a full brand + content kit to start fast (scripts, graphics, templates, positioning)

**How to apply**
Reply with: (1) an account or product you grew + the numbers, (2) 2–3 sentences on what
you'd do in your first 2 weeks for Porfilr, (3) your rate. Please start your reply with the
word "PORFILR" so I know you read this.

## KPIs (put these in the contract)

Judge on outcomes, not activity. Primary metrics (measured via our analytics + events table):
- **Signups / week** (the north star)
- **Website visits / week** (real humans, bot-excluded)
- **Email list growth / week**
- **Engaged reach** — comments/replies/DMs started, not just impressions

Secondary / leading indicators:
- Posting consistency (agreed cadence hit)
- Communities engaged (Reddit/IH/etc. per week)
- Email campaigns sent + open/click rates

NOT KPIs (ignore these as success measures): follower count alone, likes, impressions.

Set a simple target for the trial month, e.g. "grow weekly signups by X% and add Y emails."
Review against the analytics dashboard at the end of the trial.

## Screening test (filters posters from growth people — send to shortlist)

Keep it short (should take ~30 min). A poster will give generic fluff; a growth person will
be specific and metric-driven.

**Task:**
"Porfilr helps freelancers build a portfolio in 10 minutes, no code, free to start. We get
mostly mobile traffic, our audience is designers/developers/writers, and our goal is signups
(not likes). In a short reply:
1. Which ONE channel would you prioritize first for us, and why?
2. Give 3 concrete post/engagement ideas you'd run in week one (not 'post consistently' — actual ideas).
3. How would you measure whether it's working, and what would make you kill it?"

**What good looks like:**
- Picks one channel with a *reason tied to our audience/goal* (not "all of them")
- Ideas are specific and engagement-driven, not just "post the graphics"
- Talks about signups/CTR/conversion, and is willing to *kill* what doesn't work

**Red flags:**
- Talks only about followers/likes/"going viral"
- Vague ("stay consistent, use hashtags, engage with audience") with no specifics
- Can't name a single metric they'd track beyond reach

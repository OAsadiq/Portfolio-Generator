# Porfilr Trader Kit — Founding Launch content pack

Everything here drives to **porfilr.com/trader-kit** (the founding page with the live
"X of 20 spots" counter + email capture). Goal of this push: fill the 20 founding spots
and grow the email list.

## The one message everything ladders up to
**"A track record that updates itself."**
Screenshots are easy to fake and easy to ignore. Log your trades and Porfilr builds a
clean, credible page that recomputes your return, win rate, drawdown and equity curve —
and shows the day you last traded. That's proof a screenshot can never give.

## Honesty guardrails (do not cross — they protect credibility)
- Never say "verified by Porfilr." We compute from what the trader logs; we don't audit it.
- The honest edge is *"live / updates itself / shows when you last traded"* + it links to
  the trader's own verified source (MyFXBook, broker). Say that, not "verified."
- Use first names only for any real trader. Never post someone's numbers without consent.

## 1. Hacker News — Show HN
Post at: news.ycombinator.com/submit · Title format matters. No emojis, no hype.

**Title:**
Show HN: Porfilr – Build a portfolio site in 10 minutes, no code

**URL:** https://porfilr.com

**First comment (post immediately after submitting):**
Hi HN. I built Porfilr because I kept losing freelance work — not because my work was bad, but because the only thing I could send was a Google Doc of "selected work." The alternatives all had friction: Webflow took a weekend, Squarespace was $200+/year, so most freelancers just don't have a real portfolio.

Porfilr lets you pick a template, fill in your work, and publish — live in about 10 minutes. No code, fully responsive. Free to build and publish; Pro is a one-time $19 (custom domain + analytics), not a subscription. Every portfolio has a built-in contact form so enquiries go straight to the owner.

Stack: React/Vite front end, serverless API on Vercel, Supabase (Postgres + storage + auth), Resend for email.

It works for any field — designers, developers, photographers, writers, traders. I'd genuinely value feedback on the templates and the builder. What would make this a no-brainer for your portfolio?

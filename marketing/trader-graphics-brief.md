# Porfilr — Trader Kit graphics brief (for the designer)

Brand: Porfilr orange (#ea580c) as the primary accent. For traders, lean into a
**premium, credible, "serious money" aesthetic** — dark backgrounds, gold/green accents,
candlestick charts, clean equity curves. Traders are pitching investors, so it must look
trustworthy and high-end, not playful. Bold sans headlines (Inter), editorial serif for
emphasis is fine. Always include the Porfilr logo + www.porfilr.com. Double-check spelling:
"Porfilr" / "porfilr.com".
Format: vertical (4:5) + square (1:1) for social.

The positioning to convey across all of these:
**"MyFXBook is your proof. Porfilr is your pitch."** — a verified track record turned into a
credible page that gets traders funded and clients.

---

## 1. "Stop sending screenshots" (pain hook — lead with this)
- **Headline:** Stop sending screenshots.
- **Subhead:** Screenshots are easy to fake — and investors know it. Turn your verified track record into a page they'll actually trust.
- **Visual:** a messy cropped P&L screenshot crossed out → a clean, professional track-record page beside it. Dark bg, orange accent.

## 2. "Get funded. Get clients. Get taken seriously."
- **Headline:** Get funded. Get clients. Get taken seriously.
- **Subhead:** One credible page with your verified track record, strategy, and results — built in minutes.
- **Visual:** clean equity curve rising, gold/green candles, premium dark look.

## 3. Positioning — "Your proof, your pitch"
- **Headline:** MyFXBook is your proof. Porfilr is your pitch.
- **Subhead:** Link your verified results, present them beautifully, and share one link investors trust.
- **Visual:** split — raw data/verification on one side, a polished portfolio page on the other.

## 4. "Your track record deserves better than a screenshot"
- **Headline:** Your track record deserves better than a screenshot.
- **Subhead:** A professional page that proves your consistency to investors and prop firms.
- **Visual:** elegant, editorial. A framed equity curve, premium feel.

## 5. Feature — verified credibility
- **Headline:** Verified. Professional. Trusted.
- **Subhead:** Show your returns, drawdown, and strategy with a verified badge that builds instant trust.
- **Visual:** a portfolio hero with a "✔ Verified track record" badge highlighted.

## 6. Feature — turns your page into deal flow
- **Headline:** Turn your results into deal flow.
- **Subhead:** Every page has a built-in contact form — so investors and clients reach you directly.
- **Visual:** a track-record page with an "Invest with me" / "Work with me" message notification.

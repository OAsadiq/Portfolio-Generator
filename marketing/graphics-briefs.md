# Porfilr — Promo graphics briefs (for the designer)

Brand: dark backgrounds + white text + orange accent (#ea580c). Bold, confident, a little
punchy — never corporate. Fonts: Playfair Display (display) + Inter (body). Always include
the Porfilr logo + www.porfilr.com. Double-check spelling ("Porfilr", "porfilr.com").
Format: vertical (4:5) or square (1:1) for social.

---

## 1. "Why aren't you using Porfilr yet?" (FOMO / objection-buster)
- **Headline:** Why aren't you using Porfilr yet?
- **Subhead:** No code. 10 minutes. Free to start. You're out of excuses.
- **Visual:** bold headline, then the three "excuses" crossed out (~~too hard~~ ~~no time~~ ~~too expensive~~) with orange checks replacing them.
- **Use:** conversion post, pinned, ads.

## 2. Referral — "Bring friends. Get Pro free."
- **Headline:** Refer friends. Earn Pro — free.
- **Subhead:** Share your link. When friends go Pro, you do too. Everyone wins.
- **Visual:** a simple 1 → 2 → 3 arrow flow ending in an orange "PRO unlocked" badge. Warm, rewarding feel.
- **Use:** post to existing users; dashboard promo; anytime post-launch.

## 3. Objection-busters — "No excuses" grid
- **Headline:** "But I don't have…"
- **Subhead (as a list):** …coding skills → not needed. …time → 10 minutes. …money → free to start. …a designer → built in.
- **Visual:** clean 2x2 grid, each excuse struck through with the orange answer beside it.
- **Use:** carousel slide or standalone.

## 4. Comparison — "$19 once. Not $300 a year."
- **Headline:** $19 once. Not $300 a year.
- **Subhead:** Squarespace charges yearly, forever. Porfilr Pro is a one-time $19. Yours for good.
- **Visual:** side-by-side price tags — a recurring "$300/yr 🔁" vs a bold "$19 once ✓".
- **Use:** pricing/value post.

## 5. Insight — "The best work doesn't win."
- **Headline:** The best work doesn't win. The best-*presented* work does.
- **Subhead:** Fix your presentation. porfilr.com
- **Visual:** editorial, big serif headline, minimal. Quote-card energy — highly shareable.
- **Use:** thought-leadership / shareable post.

## 6. Feature — contact form ("brings you work")
- **Headline:** A portfolio that brings you work — not just shows it.
- **Subhead:** Every Porfilr site has a built-in contact form. Enquiries land straight in your inbox.
- **Visual:** a portfolio with a message notification popping in ("New enquiry ✉️").
- **Use:** feature spotlight.

## 7. Feature — one link ("everywhere")
- **Headline:** One link. Every pitch, DM, and application.
- **Subhead:** Stop scattering YouTube links, Google Docs, and dead Behance pages. One clean URL.
- **Visual:** one orange link pill in the center, arrows radiating to LinkedIn, IG, email, résumé icons.
- **Use:** feature/positioning.

## 8. "Stop losing gigs" (pain, extends the Google Docs one)
- **Headline:** You're losing gigs to people who aren't better than you.
- **Subhead:** They just present better. Fix that in 10 minutes → porfilr.com
- **Visual:** dark, a little confrontational. Bold headline, small punchy subhead.
- **Use:** scroll-stopper.

## 9. First impression / time ("10 seconds")
- **Headline:** Clients judge you in 10 seconds.
- **Subhead:** What are they seeing — a Google Doc, or a real portfolio?
- **Visual:** a stopwatch / 10s countdown motif in orange.
- **Use:** pain-point hook.

## 10. How it works (3 steps — also needed for launch carousel)
- **Headline:** Live in 3 steps.
- **Steps:** 1) Pick a template  2) Add your work  3) Publish — you're live
- **Subhead:** No code. ~10 minutes. Free to start.
- **Visual:** clean numbered 1-2-3, with a mini template → filled → live-link progression.
- **Use:** explainer; launch carousel; PH gallery.

## 11. Testimonial template (fill when reviews come in)
- **Layout:** big orange quotation mark, the quote in serif, then name + role + tiny avatar.
- **Note:** leave as a reusable template — drop in real user quotes post-launch.
- **Use:** social proof (highest-converting once you have users).

## 12. Launch announcement ("We're live")
- **Headline:** We're live. 🟠
- **Subhead:** Build a portfolio that gets you hired — free, no code, 10 minutes. porfilr.com
- **Visual:** celebratory, bold, the logo front and center.
- **Use:** launch day (July 2).

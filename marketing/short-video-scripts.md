# Porfilr — Short-form video scripts (Reels / TikTok / Shorts)

Post the SAME video to all 3 platforms. Vertical 9:16. Hook in first 2 seconds.
Keep each under 30s. On-screen text matters more than audio (most watch muted).

---

## Short 1 — "Stop sending Google Docs" (pain hook) · ~18s
Use: marketing/short-1.html (screen-record it) OR film with your graphics.

[0–2s] BIG TEXT: "Stop sending Google Docs as your portfolio."
(voiceover/caption) "If this is your portfolio, you're losing clients."

[2–7s] "A Google Doc isn't a portfolio. A LinkedIn page isn't either."
Show the messy-doc → clean-portfolio flip.

[7–13s] "Porfilr turns your work into a real site. No code. 10 minutes."
Show a finished portfolio.

[13–18s] CTA: "Free to start → porfilr.com"

Caption to post with it:
Stop sending Google Docs as your portfolio 🛑 Build a real one free in 10 min → porfilr.com
#freelance #portfolio #graphicdesign #buildinpublic

---

## Short 2 — "Portfolio in 10 minutes" (demo/how) · ~25s
Best version: SCREEN-RECORD the actual app doing this. Most convincing.

[0–2s] BIG TEXT: "Build a portfolio in 10 minutes. Watch."
[2–8s] Screen: pick a template.
[8–16s] Screen: fill in name, work, services (speed up 2–4x).
[16–22s] Screen: hit publish → the live portfolio link appears.
[22–25s] CTA: "That's it. Free → porfilr.com"

Caption:
From zero to a live portfolio in 10 minutes — no code, no designer. Try it free → porfilr.com
#portfolio #freelancer #webdesign #nocode

## Short 3 — "Why your portfolio costs you clients" (educational) · ~20s
Talking-to-camera OR text-over-graphics. This one builds authority.

[0–3s] "You're not losing clients because your work is bad."
[3–9s] "You're losing them because of how you present it. People judge in 10 seconds."
[9–15s] "A clean portfolio site beats a doc, a dead Behance link, or a LinkedIn page — every time."
[15–20s] "Build one free in 10 minutes → porfilr.com"

Caption:
The best work doesn't win. The best-presented work wins. Fix your presentation → porfilr.com
#freelance #personalbranding #portfolio #creators

---

## Posting tips
- Re-post each short 2–3 times over weeks with different hooks/captions. Short-form rewards reposting.
- TikTok/Reels search is SEO too — put "portfolio for [designers/developers]" in the caption.
- Pin your best-performing one to your profile.
- Link in bio → the matching niche landing page, not just the homepage.
